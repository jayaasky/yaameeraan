About Yaa Meeraan

Founded for inclusive spiritual guidance

Yaa Meeraan ORG was founded by Baba Ahamed Kabeer Shattari, a spiritual meditation and pilgrimage guider, in 2015.

By using the power of God, the organization shares guidance to improve business prospects, support personal-life challenges such as marriage and studies, and help people move toward a prosperous life.

Its service is inclusive so all people can benefit, regardless of race, religion, and language.

  • Founded in 2015
  • Based in Kalmunai, Sri Lanka
  • Guidance available for English, Sinhala, and Tamil audiences
Services
