Get To Know Us

Founded for inclusive spiritual guidance.

Yaa Meeraan ORG was founded by Baba Ahamed Kabeer Shattari, a spiritual meditation and pilgrimage guider, in 2015.

The organization welcomes people regardless of race, religion, and language, with guidance focused on meditation, spiritual support, and personal well-being.

Original Details

About Yaa Meeraan in three languages.

By using the power of God we improve your business prospects, help you to overcome the problems in personal life such as marriage and studies caused by negative forces, and we provide a prosperous life.

Furthermore, we can guide you to receive the power and proximity of God in your life through deep meditation.

Our service is inclusive in a way that all people can benefit from, regardless of race, religion, and language.
